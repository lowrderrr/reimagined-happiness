The preservation of the Casa de Vidro

stems from the plans of the couple Lina Bo and Pietro Maria Bardi.

In the last years of their active life, \n the couple began the process of declaring the building a historical heritage site,\n demanded at the Council for the Defense of the Historical, Archaeological,\n Artistic and Tourist Heritage (Condephaat, 1986), and founded a non-profit cultural \n association, the Bardi Institute (1990), which would become the owner of the house after \n the death of Lina and Pietro respectively in 1992 and 1999
